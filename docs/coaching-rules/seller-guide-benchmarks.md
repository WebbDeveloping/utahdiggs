## V1 scope — **keep aligned**

## Verbal benchmarks (live copy)

| Topic | Heuristic |
| --- | --- |
| Saves → showings | If saves don’t convert within **1–2 weeks**, pricing may be the barrier |
| Showings (early) | **5+** / week in first two weeks often → offers at/above list |
| Showings per offer | Healthy **7–10** |
| No contract | **10+** showings with no contract → price often the issue |
| Price watch | **10+** showings no offers; **&lt; 8** showings in **2 weeks**; **21+** DOM no contract |

## Relationship to Overview

Price review verdict uses **21+ DOM or 10+ showings** (plus 0 pending offers) — same spirit as Guide. Funnel live widget is **not** built; Guide still explains the funnel in words.
